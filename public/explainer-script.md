# Production Notes

## Visual Style
- **Color palette:** Indigo (#4F46E5), slate, emerald, amber — matches the app
- **Animation style:** Smooth 2D motion graphics with flat characters (similar to Framer Motion website style)
- **Characters:** Simple, diverse African characters (students in uniform, teachers, admin staff)
- **Screen mockups:** Device frames (laptop, tablet, phone) with real app screenshots animated inside

## Recommended Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| **Animaker** | DIY animated explainer with templates | Free/Paid |
| **Vyond** | Professional animated video | Paid ($50+/mo) |
| **Canva Video** | Simple animated scenes with stock elements | Free/Pro |
| **After Effects** | Premium custom animation | Paid |
| **Fiverr / Upwork** | Hire a freelance explainer video creator | $200–$800 |

## Voiceover
- **Style:** Warm, confident, African-accent English (or local language dub)
- **Recommendation:** Hire a VO artist on Fiverr (~$50–$100)
- **Duration:** ~350 words, ~2:30 at moderate pace

## Music Suggestions
- *"Uplifting Africa"* by Audiojungle
- *"Inspiring Afrobeat"* by Pond5
- *"Positive Energy"* by Artlist
- Or search: "upbeat afrobeat corporate" on any stock music site

## If Budget is Limited (DIY Approach)
1. Use **Canva** with their animated characters and scene transitions
2. Record voiceover with phone or free tool like Audacity
3. Use **CapCut** (free) to sync audio + visuals
4. Total cost: $0–$20 (stock music license)
